# **📊 HR Employee Attrition Analysis & Prediction**

Power BI and Python project analyzing employee attrition and predicting workforce trends

## **📌 Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition using Power BI and Python to identify key factors influencing employee turnover. The goal is to derive actionable insights that can help organizations improve employee retention and workforce management.

The project combines data visualization, exploratory data analysis, and predictive modeling to understand patterns behind employee attrition.

## **🎯 Business Objective**

- Identify key factors contributing to employee attrition
- Analyze employee behavior across departments and roles
- Understand the impact of salary, satisfaction, and experience
- Build a predictive model to estimate attrition risk
  
##**📂 Dataset Description**

The dataset contains employee-level information such as:

- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Monthly Income
- Job Satisfaction
- Years at Company
- Education Field
- Attrition (Yes/No)
  
## **🧮 Data Preparation**

- Converted categorical attrition values into numeric format
- Cleaned and transformed dataset
- Created calculated measures in Power BI
- Handled missing values and formatted numerical data
  
## **📊 Power BI Dashboard**

### **🔹 Key Metrics**

- Total Employees: 1470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16%
- Average Salary: 6.5K
  
### **🔹 Visualizations**

- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Salary vs Attrition
- Job Satisfaction vs Attrition
- Years at Company vs Attrition
- Age Distribution of Employees
  
### **📈 Key Insights**

- Employees with lower job satisfaction show higher attrition
- Attrition is highest in early years of employment
- Certain job roles experience significantly higher turnover
- Lower salary levels are associated with higher attrition
- Younger employees tend to leave more frequently

## 🤖 Machine Learning (Python)

A Logistic Regression model was used to predict employee attrition.

### Features Used:
- Age
- Monthly Income
- Job Satisfaction
- Years at Company
  
### Outcome:
- Model predicts likelihood of employee attrition
- Helps identify high-risk employees
